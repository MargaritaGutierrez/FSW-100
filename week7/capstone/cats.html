Cats For Adoption

Cats who are a fit for your family can be trained for emotional support and service. High anxiety and disacociation is somethings that cats are great to have around for. With minor training cats can be a very good pet for minor mental emotional needs.




Things to take into consideration when adopting a new furry family member!
Cats are helpful for?
Emotional support, they may not want to be played with all the time but with our training they will be there with you when you need them more than they need you.

    What you will need before adoption:

  • If any doctor recommendations apply, please have ready the exact requirements.
  • Please have your ID ready for an animal/vet background check. (This is to ensure the pet is going to a save home with no history of abuse or neglect)
  • Please have your food of choice determined. (this will help us arrange the smoothest transition to placement as possible)
  • Having a planned future date to train with us on how to utalize your new pets ques priorr to taking the pet home is a must.
  • Make sure you bring all the love and support you have so others can learn the pets ques in order to support you with your new pet!
